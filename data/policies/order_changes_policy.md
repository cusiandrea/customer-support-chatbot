# Order Change Policy

We understand that customers may need to modify their orders after placing them. Our Order Change Policy outlines the conditions under which changes can be requested.

Orders can be modified within a limited time window after confirmation. Typically, this window lasts up to 2 hours from the time the order is placed. After this period, orders may already be processed or prepared for shipment and therefore cannot be altered.

Customers may request changes such as:
- Updating shipping address
- Correcting contact details
- Modifying product quantities or variants

To request a change, customers must contact our support team via email or customer service form, including their order number and requested modifications.

Please note:
- Changes are not guaranteed and depend on the order processing stage.
- Price adjustments may apply if items are added or replaced.
- If a modification is not possible, customers may request cancellation (subject to approval).

We reserve the right to decline modification requests once fulfillment has begun.

For urgent requests, we recommend contacting support immediately after placing the order.
