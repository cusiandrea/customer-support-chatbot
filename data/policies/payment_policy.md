# Payment Policy

We offer secure and convenient payment options to ensure a smooth checkout experience.

Accepted payment methods include:
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
- Digital wallets (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Other local payment methods where available

All payments must be completed at the time of purchase. Orders will not be processed or shipped until payment has been successfully authorized.

We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect customer data. We do not store full payment details on our servers.

In case of payment failure:
- The order will not be confirmed
- Customers may retry using a different payment method

Prices are displayed in the selected currency and include applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. Additional charges such as shipping fees will be clearly indicated before checkout.

We reserve the right to cancel orders suspected of fraud or unauthorized transactions. In such cases, customers will be notified and refunded if applicable.

For billing issues or discrepancies, customers should contact support within 7 days of the transaction date.
