# Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering orders efficiently and reliably.

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. Processing times may vary during peak periods or promotional events.

We offer the following shipping options:
- Standard shipping (estimated delivery: 3–7 business days)
- Express shipping (estimated delivery: 1–3 business days)

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination, weight, and selected shipping method.

Customers will receive a tracking number once the order has been shipped. Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by external factors such as customs delays or courier issues.

We currently ship to selected regions. Availability will be shown during checkout.

Important notes:
- Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information
- We are not liable for delays caused by incorrect addresses
- Customs duties and taxes (if applicable) are the responsibility of the customer

If a package is lost or delayed, customers should contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date.

For further assistance, please reach out to our customer service team.
