# Refund Policy

Our Refund Policy ensures fair handling of customer claims while maintaining operational integrity.

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:
- Returned items that meet our return conditions
- Orders canceled before shipment
- Defective or incorrect products received

Refunds are processed using the original payment method unless otherwise agreed. Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider, typically ranging from 5 to 10 business days after approval.

To be eligible for a refund:
- The request must be submitted within 14 days of delivery
- The item must comply with our Return Policy

Non-refundable cases include:
- Final sale or clearance items
- Gift cards
- Products damaged due to misuse

If a refund is approved, customers will receive confirmation via email. Shipping costs are generally non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

We reserve the right to deny refund requests that do not meet policy conditions.

For assistance, customers should contact our support team with relevant order details and documentation.
