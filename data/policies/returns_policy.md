# Returns Policy

We aim to provide a hassle-free return experience for our customers.

Customers may return eligible items within 14 days of receiving their order. To qualify for a return, items must:
- Be unused and in original condition
- Include original packaging and tags
- Be accompanied by proof of purchase

To initiate a return:
1. Contact customer support with your order number
2. Receive return authorization and instructions
3. Ship the item back using the provided guidelines

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item.

Non-returnable items include:
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as final sale

Once the return is received and inspected, we will notify the customer of the outcome. Approved returns may result in a refund or exchange.

We reserve the right to reject returns that do not meet the above conditions.

For any questions, please contact our support team before sending items back.
